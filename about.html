Laka Battle Royale!!!!

Do you want to play as a Lakamon? Do you want to use abilities to get to a Laka Victory Royale? DO YOU WANT TO FIGHT TO THE DEATH? Then Lakamon Battle Royale is a game for you. We thought: "Why play the trainer if you can play as the Lakamon? It would be way more fun and cool if you could." So for the fist time ever, there is a game where you can play as the Lakamon and fight against other Lakamons for a Lakamon Victory Royale. To win in the game you have to survive in a big arena while the walls are becomming smaller. There are items that you can pick up and use to upgrade your stats. We have 3 very cute Lakamon for you to play, each with their own abilities and type. You want to make Waves? Charmandolphin is just for you. You want to breath fire? Take the devilish vulcasaur. You want to shoot weird objectiles made from grass? Then select McQuirtle (hamburger included). So what are you waiting for? Get your friends, start your own server and WIN!
